About

Hi, I am Dr. Ravikiran Yeleswarapu

An Assistant Professor specializing in Computer Engineering at the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. My research interests lie in computer architecture, embedded systems, and human-centric technologies.

Specifically, I explore topics such as memory, reliability, power, IoT applications, and the intersection of technology and mental health.

I am dedicated to fostering innovation and inspiring the next generation of engineers. Through a combination of academic rigor and real-world insights, I aim to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to tackle today's engineering challenges.
